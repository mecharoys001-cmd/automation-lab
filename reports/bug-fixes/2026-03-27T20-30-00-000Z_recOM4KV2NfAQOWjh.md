## Feedback
The role management system had architectural gaps where the 4-tier role model (Master Admin, Admin, Editor, Staff) was partially enforced. While the 'editor' DB enum existed and ~30 API routes used `requireMinRole('standard')`, several critical routes lacked enforcement and the Staff/Instructor tier had no API-level access for the portal.

---

## Analysis
The editor role enum was added to the DB (migration 20260316) and ~30 routes enforced it, but several critical routes only called `requireAdmin()` without role-level checks. The instructor portal APIs used `requireAdmin()` which blocked actual instructors entirely. The middleware and sidebar only restricted a few pages for editors while leaving most management pages accessible.
